Villa Serenity

Soft coastal villa interior defined by warm minimalism, low volumes and quiet connection to the sea.

Villa Serenity is designed as a calm retreat where light timber, sand-toned textiles and refined details frame expansive views of the coastline. Spaces flow effortlessly from terrace to living room to bedroom, keeping the horizon always in sight.

The palette is deliberately restrained, combining soft textures, gentle light and precise joinery. Each vignette is composed to feel effortless and lived-in, supporting slow mornings, long evenings and an overall sense of ease.
