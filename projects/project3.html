Wabi-Sabi house

Soft, restrained architecture celebrating patina, texture and quiet everyday rituals.

The Wabi-Sabi house explores rich timber tones, layered lighting and soft acoustic textiles. Spaces are kept simple and open, allowing natural materials and changing light to define the mood.

The palette balances depth and warmth while maintaining clarity of form, creating a home that feels calm, tactile and lived-in rather than polished.
