EcoCapsules

A modular tower system that adapts to any climate and community, blending flexibility and sustainability in one vertical neighborhood.

EcoCapsules is based on the NeoCapsules modular building system, a residential solution that can be tuned to different climates, densities and urban conditions. Inspired by the Nakagin Capsule Tower and developed at @iaac_macad, the project reimagines capsule living for a contemporary, sustainable city.

The tower is assembled from prefabricated capsules that can be added, removed or reconfigured over time. A parametric envelope responds to orientation, sun, wind and local climate, while shared terraces, green decks and a highly active ground floor create a cohesive community from a flexible kit of parts.

Project Details
Type Architecture · Modular Building System

Key advantages: modules can be added or removed as needs change; parametric design adapts to climate and location; green shared spaces and rooftop gardens; an active ground hub connecting daily life to the city; and energy-efficient, eco-friendly construction for a future-proof tower.
